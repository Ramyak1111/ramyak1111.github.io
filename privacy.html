How this portfolio handles contact information

Last updated: April 1, 2026

This website is a personal portfolio for Ramya Kannoji. If you contact Ramya through the email address, phone number, resume link, or any form added later, the information you share is expected to be used only for professional communication related to hiring, networking, or career opportunities.

Information that may be collected

Depending on how the site is hosted or updated later, the website may collect basic contact details such as your name, email address, phone number, organization, and message. Standard server logs may also record technical details such as browser type, IP address, and access times.

How the information may be used

  • To respond to job-related or professional inquiries.
  • To share follow-up information about opportunities or conversations already requested.
  • To maintain normal communication records for professional correspondence.

Data sharing

Personal information should not be sold. If third-party services are added later for hosting, forms, or analytics, those services may process limited technical or contact data as part of operating the website.

Retention

Messages and related contact information may be kept only as long as needed for professional follow-up, record keeping, or opportunity tracking.

Your choices

If you would like your contact information updated or removed from communication records, please reach out using the contact information listed on the portfolio home page.
